Official network channels

#SwiftIRC - This is SwiftIRC's main network channel. Although it has no specific role, it is used as a general discussion channel.

#Help - #Help Wiki Page - This is the SwiftIRC's main support channel. Users should use this channel for any general questions, or IRC-related queries they may have (such as services commands, issues with users/channels, or homework help).

#Support - #Support Wiki Page - Intended for use by users who require administrative support. It is advisable that you join #Help beforehand - If necessary, you will be directed to #support by a helper.

#Wiki - This is SwiftIrc's main channel for maintaining SwiftIrc's Wiki. Questions about the pages or help us fix errors.

Recent network news

April 9th 2014 - Fully Linked: light.wa.us.SwiftIRC.net

I'm pleased to announce that the server light.wa.us.SwiftIRC.net has completed its 30 day trial and passed a vote by the SwiftIRC Administration for a permanent link.

We extend our congratulations to Light's admin Benjy and warmly welcome him to the Server Administrator team.

February 11th 2014 - Light.wa.us.SwiftIRC.net Granted Test Link

light.wa.us.SwiftIRC.net has been granted a 30 day test link to the network. We wish its owner, Benjy, best of luck on his trial.

In other news, energy.de.eu.SwiftIRC.net has been fully linked to the network. Congratulations to its administrator Sony for passing his trial!

January 7th 2014 - Energy.de.eu.SwiftIRC.net Granted Test Link

I would like to announce that energy.de.eu.SwiftIRC.net has been granted a 30 day test link to the network.

We wish it's owner, sony, best of luck on his trial.

21st December 2013 - Server Updates

lunas.fr.eu.swiftirc.net and tripwire.uk.eu.swiftirc.net have been delinked from the network. We would like to thank their administrators, Redzzy and IEP respectively for their countless hours of service to SwiftIRC.

On a more positive note, atlas.fr.eu.SwiftIRC.net has been fully linked to the network. Congratulations to its administrator eTch AKA Bluebus112 for passing his trial!
